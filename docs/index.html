Why Visualinks?

Tired of tab chaos and messy bookmarks? Visualinks helps you organize Chrome visually.

  • 🎨 Organize bookmarks with icons, images & GIFs
  • 📂 Group links into Rooms & Stations
  • 🔍 Search across bookmarks, history & tabs instantly
  • 🧱 Customize layouts & backgrounds
  • 🔐 100% local storage – privacy-first

The Story Behind Visualinks

Visualinks was born out of late-night frustration with endless tabs and scattered bookmarks. Instead of text lists, Visualinks turns your browser into a visual hub that matches how your brain remembers things.
