The Story Behind Visualinks

Tired of drowning in Chrome tabs and messy bookmarks? Visualinks is a visual bookmark manager and tab organizer that turns your browser into a customizable workspace. Instead of scrolling through endless text lists, you can navigate your bookmarks and links visually, intuitively, and memorably.

Transform your browser into a visually organized hub of your favorite links using custom backgrounds, icons, and GIFs. This Chrome extension allows you to build personalized, memorable “Rooms” where each category or group of links is clearly distinguished by visuals that make navigation fast, intuitive, and enjoyable.

Instead of sifting through endless bookmarks or tabs, you can create interactive spaces:

  • Items hold your individual links.
  • Stations act as portals to new views or categories.
  • Dummies are decorative image holders to personalize your space.

Key Features:

  • 🎨 Visual Organization: Assign unique icons, images, or GIFs to links and categories to easily recognize and recall their purpose.
  • 🧠 Memory-Based Navigation: Create link groups in ways that align with how you naturally remember information—visually and spatially.
  • 🔍 Smart Search: Instantly search across your saved links, Chrome bookmarks, history, and reading list—even by typing just a few letters or keywords.
  • 🗂 Hierarchical Structure: Group related links under categories (e.g., shopping 🛒, projects, research) and navigate through a multi-level system of rooms and stations.
  • 🧱 Customizable Layout: Change backgrounds, assign transparent icons, and visually design each space to your liking.
  • 📁 Supports Local Files: Link to local PDF files (when using Adobe Acrobat extension).
  • 🔐 Privacy-First: No Google account required. No data is sent to the cloud. Everything is stored locally in your browser and can be exported/imported for backup.
  • 🗑 Trash System: Deleted items go to trash and can be reviewed before permanent removal.

Get Started: Install Visualinks today and turn your browser into a visual hub for everything you do online.

Usage Instructions (watch the video for step-by-step instructions):

  • Right-click on any view to access actions like opening bookmarks, history, tabs, or reading list.
  • Add links to items manually or by dragging from other Chrome dialogs.
  • Hover over items to preview saved links.
  • Assign icons individually to distinguish between similar links, even from the same domain.
  • Customize every object (Item, Station, Dummy) with a title and a public image URL.
  • Use search across all views to quickly access a link or jump to a specific room (available unless a dialog/context menu is open).
  • Stations cannot be deleted without removing their children first.
  • Objects and links are removed permanently once deleted from trash.

Backup & Portability:
Export your workspace from the options menu for backup or transfer to another device. Note: If the extension is uninstalled without exporting, your data will be lost.
